Research Interests & Areas of Expertise

  • Search Relevance and Ranking: Developing and fine-tuning ranking algorithms that incorporate textual relevance signals, business metrics, and user behavior data.
  • Offline Evaluation & A/B Testing: Establishing rigorous, repeatable offline evaluation frameworks as a cost-effective and low-risk alternative to live A/B testing for search algorithm validation.
  • Information Retrieval Metrics: Practical application and analysis of rank-aware metrics such as nDCG, MAP, and MRR to quantify search quality.
  • LLMs for Search Evaluation: Utilizing Large Language Models (LLMs) to automate and scale the creation of ground-truth data (judgement lists) and to perform qualitative analysis of search results.
  • Query Understanding and Analysis: Employing techniques for query clustering, typo-tolerance, and semantic analysis to better comprehend user intent.

Projects

Founder & Chief Architect, TestMySearch.com

TestMySearch is a comprehensive, engine-agnostic platform I created for search quality analysis and optimization. It provides a systematic, data-driven workflow for relevance engineers to evaluate and compare different search configurations. The platform operationalizes many of my core research interests.

Key Features:

  • An integrated A/B testing platform for running thousands of queries against multiple search backends (e.g., Solr, Elasticsearch) in offline batch runs.
  • Automated calculation and visualization of a wide array of IR metrics.
  • A "Virtual Search Assessor" that leverages LLMs to evaluate document-to-query relevance and generate new test queries.
