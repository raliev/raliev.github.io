From Data Overload to Clear Conclusions

After your tests have run, TestMySearch provides a powerful suite of reporting tools designed to help you quickly understand the results and make confident, data-backed decisions. No more wading through spreadsheetsâ€”just clear, actionable insights.

Side-by-Side Comparison

Select any runs for a specific query and instantly see a comparison view. This table shows the rank of each document across different configurations, immediately highlighting how rankings change and which version surfaces relevant documents higher.

Detailed Metric Reports

Generate comprehensive reports that aggregate metrics across all queries. These reports include per-query breakdowns, statistical summaries (mean, median, std dev), and pairwise win/loss/tie counts to clearly identify the superior configuration.

Interactive Visualizations

Each report is more than just a table of numbers. It's an interactive tool. Click on a column to sort the data and watch as the accompanying charts are rebuilt in real-time. This allows you to explore the data from different angles and truly understand the "why" behind the performance differences.
