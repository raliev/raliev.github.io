Visualize your data and track the key performance indicators that matter.

Telling a Story with Data

The final phase of our search analytics methodology is to transform your processed data into compelling visualizations and trackable Key Performance Indicators (KPIs). The goal is to make it easy for stakeholders across your organization to understand search performance at a glance and make data-driven decisions.

Essential Search KPIs

  • Conversion Rate by Query: Which search terms are most likely to lead to a purchase?
  • Zero-Result Search Rate: What percentage of searches are returning no results?
  • Average Click Position: On average, how far down the results page are users clicking?
  • Search Adoption Rate: What percentage of your users are engaging with the search feature?
