A Practical Blueprint

500+ pages of concentrated knowledge—no fluff.
Based on 25 years of experience, this book bridges the gap between academic theory and the complex reality of building search engines that actually sell. It structures knowledge often ignored by existing literature, specifically catering to the unique challenges of e-commerce.

Technical & Architectural Deep Dive: From IR basics and microservices to decoding large-scale solutions from giants like Amazon, eBay, and Shopify.
